Section 13.46 of the Minnesota Statutes pertains to **Welfare Data**. Here are some key points:

1. **Definitions**: The section defines terms such as "individual," "program," "welfare system," "mental health data," "fugitive felon," and "private licensing agency".

2. **General Classification**: Data on individuals collected, maintained, used, or disseminated by the welfare system is considered private data. It cannot be disclosed except under specific conditions, such as court orders, statutes authorizing access, or for investigations related to the administration of welfare programs.
